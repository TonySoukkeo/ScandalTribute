This is Scandal!

Scandal is an all female Japanese rock band originating from Osaka, Japan. They all met in a vocal and dance school, CALESS. Shortly thereafter, they have formed their group in August of 2006. Starting out at first playing live street perfomances, they were then noticed by indie label, Kitty Records, which ultimately signed them on. Until recently, Scandal has went on and fomred their own music label, "her", on Christmas Eve 2018.

The band consists of 4 members, while all providing lead vocals at some point, their lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist is Haruna Ono,Mami Sasazaki on lead guitar, Tomomi Ogawa on bass, and Rina Suzuki on drums.

Timeline

The Girls

Haruna Ono

Lead vocalist / Rhthym guitar

Haruna is the eldest among the group and also serves as the leader. Haruna has the most powerful and deep voice of the group that usually leads in their songs combined with the voices of the other 3 members.

Mami Sasazaki

Lead Guitarist

Sasazaki Mami has the best guitar skills among the girls, and serves as the lead guitarist in Scandal. She often amazes fans that come to see them live with her dexterity and skill with the electric guitar.

Also among the group, she’s also the biggest fan of Japanese Anime.

Rina Suzuki

Drummer

Rina is the youngest among the group and serves as the drummer, she always has a smile painted on her face when performing.

Rina can also play the piano and has been playing since she was 3 years old. Rina was the last member to join the group.

Tomomi Ogawa

Bassist

Tomomi is the bass and writer of majority of SCANDAL's songs. Tomomi has a very sweet and kinda “chipmunky” voice that is easy to like, together with Haruna, their voices become a very perfect combination. Tomomi typically leads in solo parts of SCANDAL's songs.
